HOW TO PLAY

NUKO is a multiplayer first person shooter. Multiple players join a lobby. A timer counts down from 3:00 minutes as all the players battle. Each kill rewards a point. The player with the highest point by the end of the round wins.

CONTROLS

WASD - forward left back right
1 2 - pistol katana
SPACE - jump
LMB - attack
RMB - dash
Y - chat

CONSOLE COMMANDS

Console commands are denoted with the prefix '!' before a chat message


view score - !score
change name - !name [new name]
change sensitivity - !sensitivity [new sensitivity]

BASIC MECHANICS

PISTOL - Hold LMB to rapidly fire bullets. Each hit will deal 10 damage.

KATANA - Hold LMB to swing katana. One hit will kill the enemy.

DASH - By dashing with the pistol, you go backwards. By dashing with the katana you go forwards.

AIRSTRAFING

While airborne, you can strafe left or right without holding forward to control the direction of flight. This will allow you to control where you go when dashing with the katana or pistol. Here is a video tutorial of how air-strafing works

BHOPPING

By holding JUMP while landing, you automatically jump again, preserving velocity. This allows for you to continue moving without breaking momentum
